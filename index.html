PazWork

Layout-based PHP micro-framework for full-stack HTML5 sites based on hackwork https://github.com/ZDroid/hackwork but improved and powered.

PazWork is a layout-based PHP micro-framework made for HTML5 sites.

Minimal required PHP version is 5.3.0. PazWork may work on older PHP versions, but they aren't officially supported.
